MonkeyVision - Robot Uprising 2019 hackathon

RobotUprising 2019 Hackathon.

Solutions to the challenges presented in Robot Uprising 2019 Hackathon in Helsinki. Four out of five challenges were successfully solved autonomously except number two, that was performed manually. These approaches allowed our team, MonkeyVision, to be ranked 5th out of 38 teams overall.

The robot used in this hackathon is the Lego Mindstorms EV3. Our solution is implemented in MicroPython using only the infrared and the color/light intensity detector.
