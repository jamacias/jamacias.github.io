Hey there, I'm Javi

and I am deeply into robotics.

Isn't it awesome and satisfying when things work optimally on their own? That's what I thought back in the days of high school. Since then, I got involved in engineering and technology projects to make stuff do other stuff, autonomously. Naturally, my interests back then were focused in electronics and anything surrounding automation. Ever since, I love working on side projects and, while you are reading this, I might be involved in one right now. So far, I have built from an autonomous(-ish) rover for agriculture to some CNC machines and less complex IoT-related systems for monitoring.

Years on, as an engineer I keep my passion for electronics and hardware design. Nonetheless, I have discovered that I also enjoy software development and have a good set of computer science skills that already allow me to prototype from scratch all the way to the end.

For the most part it is all lately related to robotics/C++/Python/image processing stuff at the moment, but I am always down to trying new things out.
