I love robots.

My experience is not limited to my academic studies as I love doing side projects, and I might probably be involved in one right now. They are usually related to electronics and automation because I find extremely awesome and satisfying when things work on their own. So far I have built from an autonomous rover for agriculture to some CNC machines and less complex IoT-related systems for monitoring.

As an engineer, I have a natural passion for electronics and hardware design. I enjoy working with control systems, instrumentation and other core electronic topics. However, I am also interested in the application of reinforcement learning and computer vision to robotics and automation to reach optimal decisions in autonomous systems.
